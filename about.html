Our Story, Forged Over Decades

Founded in 1994, Ansam Metals Corporation began with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and efficient metal recycling services to the Baltimore community. We started with a small yard and a dedicated team, built on the principles of hard work and fair dealing.

Our roots in Baltimore are deep. We've grown alongside this resilient city, evolving our processes and expanding our reach while remaining committed to the local economy and environment. Today, we are a leading processor of non-ferrous metals, serving clients across the nation and the world, but our heart remains right here, on Patapsco Avenue.
