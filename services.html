Our Core Services

Metal Purchasing

We offer competitive, transparent pricing for all grades of non-ferrous scrap metal. Our services include on-site evaluation, certified weight measurements, and prompt payment for industrial, commercial, and demolition clients.

Processing & Sorting

Utilizing advanced sorting technology, including X-ray fluorescence (XRF) analyzers, we meticulously separate metals into clean, furnace-ready commodities. Our processes include shearing, baling, and shredding to meet precise customer specifications.

Bulk & Contract Sales

We are a trusted supplier to domestic and international foundries, mills, and manufacturers. We offer bulk quantities and long-term contracts for a reliable supply of high-quality copper, aluminum, brass, zinc, and other base metals.

Logistics & Transport

Our in-house logistics team manages the seamless transportation of materials. We provide roll-off container services for scrap collection and coordinate all freight and shipping documentation for domestic and export sales, ensuring timely delivery.
